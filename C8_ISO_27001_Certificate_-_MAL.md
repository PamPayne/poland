## Scope of Certificate

This certificate is valid for the activities specified below:

The Information Security Management System that supports the provision of services by the Continent 8 Technology Solutions teams based in the Isle of Man, Montreal, Dublin, Gibraltar, Malta and Atlantic City to external customers. All in accordance with the current version of the Statement of Applicability.

Certification of Registration remains the property of Certification Europe Ltd.

The validity of this Certificate is maintained on the condition that the Management System is assessed through an on-going surveillance programme and continues to adequately meet the requirements of the standard.
