# Poland Market Overview – May 2020

## Factor Detail

| Factor | Detail |
|---|---|
| Market Size (GGR) | Implied annual GGR of €98m in 2019; 13% YOY growth (Section 2.2) |
| Tax rate | 12% of wagering but “consumed by player” at bet placement; (2.3) |
| Licence fees | €45k - valid for 6 years (2.4) |
| Tech hurdles | Low level betslip modifications required to manage taxes (wager & winnings) & control minimum payout (4.1) |
|  | Penetration & Vulnerability testing via eCogra (4.6) |

## 1. Country / Market Background

### 1.1.
Poland has a population of 38 million with GDP/Capita of $14,901 (56th richest)

### 1.2.
Online casino is not permitted (state monopoly)

### 1.3.
Currently 18 licenced operators offering online sports betting (see Appendix 1 for full list)

### 1.4.
The market leaders are STS and Fortuna (together controlling approx. 80% of market)

### 1.5.
None of major Western European operators are licenced to operate in Poland

## 2. Commercials

### 2.1.
Known online turnover of €1,3bn in 2019 (2018 - €1,15bn)

### 2.2.
With an assumed margin of 7.5% (low due to wagering tax being consumed by player) implied 2019 online GGR market size of €98m (2018 - €86m) being a 13% YOY growth

### 2.3.
Sports tax: 12% wagering – this is consumed by the customer at the bet slip level

### 2.4.
Licence fees: €45k valid for 6 years

### 2.5.
Share capital requirement: €440k & Financial guarantee: €106k

### 2.6.
Favourable commercials given tax regime and minimal licence fees

## 3. Legal

### 3.1.
80% of licence application completed by Gevisser Law with GM Gaming as applicant

### 3.2.
Project paused in October 2019 due to BtoBet product readiness concerns

### 3.3.
No local entity or land-based partner required; but local representative is needed

### 3.4.
Bad actor provision: Organisers must not have any criminal convictions

### 3.5.
Servers can be located anywhere within the EU

### 3.6.
No Polish bank account required

### 3.7.
Betting rule changes require regulator approval before changes are made

## 4. Product / Development requirements

### 4.1.
If decision is made to use SWIX there would be 3 bet slip developments required:

#### 4.1.1.
Stake tax (12% on stake) – funded by the player (wager amount is reduced)

#### 4.1.2.
Winning tax (10% above threshold)

Payouts above PLN 2,280 are subject to a 10% tax – paid from winnings

#### 4.1.3.
Minimum winnings (payouts cannot be lower than the stake amount)

Bets resulting in winnings below the original stake, must not be accepted

### 4.2.
Derivco Sport advised that this is possible and would take approx. 2-3 sprints (1-2 months)

#### 4.2.1.
Front end – relatively simple; potentially ring fenced ‘Africa’ team can deliver

#### 4.2.2.
Core Betting – central team; betway.com timeline impact, but small

### 4.3.
Temporary accounts and relevant accounts statuses to be managed via PGSI group settings

### 4.4.
Some additional payment methods required ideally: DotPay, Blik & Tpay.com (additional research required)

### 4.5.
No SAFE server needed, but archive server required in the EU

### 4.6.
Penetration and Vulnerability testing to be done by eCogra

### 4.7.
eSports & Betgames permitted (research on Betgames regulations ongoing)
